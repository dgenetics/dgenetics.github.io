William Djinis

As a designer, I strive to understand and define root problems to arrive at elegant solutions. Simplicity comes from the hard work of sculpting away everything inessential. I try to craft delightful experiences by empathizing with end users through every interaction.

As a producer, I recruit exceptionally talented people and inspire them to do their best work. Because I genuinely care about people, I recognize their special qualities. By listening and understanding, I learn what energizes them and how to remove obstacles.

As a founder, I see how things are and can't help but imagine how they should be. Then, I throw myself into creating a better world.
